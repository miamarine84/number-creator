Marcos's 305 phone creator Version 1.0

This site is meant to create non duplicate phone numbers to be able to send mass text with the Google mass texter extension

How to use this:
  • 1. Click ADD MORE NUMBERS to be able to add more numbers to a list on our backend.

  • 2. When you would like to see all the numbers we have created for you click on Display List

  • 3. When you are ready to copy and paste click on the HIDE Everything for 5 seconds button to be able to hide all and you can copy and paste numbers in to Google mass texter extension on chrome
